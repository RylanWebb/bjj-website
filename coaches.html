Head Coach – Professor Bryan “The Anvil” Santoro

With over 20 years on the mats, Professor Bryan Santoro brings world-class Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu experience to Kami Klutch. A 3rd-degree black belt under Roberto “Cyborg” Abreu, Bryan has competed internationally and produced multiple IBJJF and ADCC medalists.
His coaching philosophy blends old-school fundamentals with modern strategy — focusing on pressure passing, submission chaining, and building a champion mindset both on and off the mats.

No-Gi Coach – Coach Devin “Reaper” Cole

Known for his relentless pace and submission-heavy style, Coach Devin Cole is a rising force in the no-gi scene. A brown belt under Professor Santoro, Devin has made his mark in promotions like Grappling Industries and Finishers Sub-Only.
His classes center around leg lock systems, scramble control, and the art of counter-attacking. Devin’s approach pushes students to stay aggressive while mastering positional awareness and defense.

Striking Coach – Coach Jason “Steel” Vega

Coach Jason Vega brings over a decade of professional striking experience from both Muay Thai and MMA competition. A former lightweight contender and certified striking instructor, Jason’s teaching blends technical precision with practical fight application.
His sessions focus on footwork, range control, and fluid combinations — turning students into confident, composed strikers. Jason believes in discipline through repetition and creativity through experience.
