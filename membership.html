Train Smart. Train Hard. Train Kami Klutch

At Kami Klutch Jiu Jitsu and MMA, every athlete starts somewhere — and we’re here to help you find the right path. Whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time or preparing for your next competition, the best way to begin is with a free trial class.

Foundation Membership

Perfect for: Beginners or anyone looking to get in shape, learn the basics, and build real confidence.

  • Up to 2 classes per week
  • Access to beginner BJJ and striking sessions
  • Open mat access on weekends
  • Gym use during staffed hours

Warrior Membership

Perfect for: Dedicated students ready to train consistently and push their skills further.

  • Unlimited BJJ (Gi & No-Gi) and striking classes
  • Access to advanced sessions & live sparring
  • Competition prep guidance
  • Discounts on seminars and private lessons

Elite Membership

Serious martial artists and competitors chasing the highest level of performance.

  • Full unlimited access to all programs
  • Personalized coaching & competition planning
  • Strength & conditioning access
  • Priority registration for seminars and workshops
